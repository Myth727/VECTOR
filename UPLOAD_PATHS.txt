WHAT'S IN THIS RELEASE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

sdk/causal.ts (NEW)
  - Trajectory-based counterfactual measurement (forwardDeltas[k], recovered)
  - computeShadowStats with policy/clean-baseline disjointness rule
  - extractRawBuckets / extractPooledDeltas for downstream stats

sdk/stats.ts (NEW)
  - Mann-Whitney U (tie-corrected, normal approx)
  - Fisher's exact test (2×2)
  - Percentile bootstrap CI (seeded, reproducible)
  - Benjamini-Hochberg FDR correction
  - compareArms + runCellTests consumers

sdk/reliability.ts (NEW)
  - Normal tier: probFailureN, totalProbability, nForTargetFailure,
    componentFailureAny, estimatePerTurnRate
  - Advanced tier (consent-gated): sodsLawScore, entropyDriftNarrative

VECTOR.jsx (UPDATED, 8,175 lines, +575 over V1.8.1)
  - Inline mirrors of all three SDK modules
  - 4 new useMemo hooks: shadowSummary, statsReport, reliabilityReport, sodsReport
  - 10 new sidebar readout rows (shadow baseline, stats, reliability)
  - Advanced-tab Sod's Law panel (6 sliders, consent-gated, satirical label)
  - New state persisted in session save/restore
